Valuation Types

Services Offered

⊕
Insurance

Insurance Valuations

Accurate replacement-value schedules prepared to the standard required by insurers. Essential for high-value jewellery, family heirlooms, and inherited pieces — ensuring your cover reflects today's market prices.

  • Individual item descriptions and replacement values
  • Photographs of each piece in the report
  • Accepted by all major UK insurers
  • Recommended for review every three to five years
⊜
Probate

Probate Valuations

Market-value assessments for HMRC probate purposes, prepared in accordance with the Inheritance Tax Act 1984. Gaynor works sensitively with families and estate professionals at what is often a difficult time.

  • Open-market valuations for HMRC compliance
  • Itemised schedules suitable for estate administration
  • Co-ordination with solicitors and executors where required
  • Discreet home visits arranged at a time that suits you
⊛
Family Division

Division of Assets & Family Division

Independent, impartial valuations for matrimonial and financial remedy proceedings. Gaynor's court-admissible reports are accepted by the Family Division and relied upon by both parties' legal teams.

  • Open-market values for fair asset division
  • Suitable for use in consent orders and Form E schedules
  • Strictly independent — no affiliation to either party
  • Report format aligned with family court expectations
⊙
Horology

Watches & Horology

Specialist valuations for watches and horological pieces across all purposes — insurance, probate, or division of assets. Gaynor's expertise covers both antique pocket watches and contemporary luxury timepieces.

  • Luxury and prestige marques: Rolex, Patek Philippe, Cartier, and others
  • Antique and vintage pocket watches
  • Dress watches and decorative timepieces
  • Values cross-referenced against current auction and retail markets

Horology

Watch & Clock
Valuations

Gaynor's horological knowledge spans several centuries of watchmaking — from Georgian and Victorian pocket watches to contemporary luxury wristwatches from the world's leading marques.

Each horological valuation is cross-referenced against current auction results, grey-market pricing, and manufacturer retail data to ensure the figure accurately reflects the present market.

⌚
Luxury wristwatches

Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Cartier, IWC, and other prestige marques valued to current market.

🕰
Antique & vintage pieces

Georgian and Victorian pocket watches, dress watches, and decorative timepieces with full provenance consideration.

📄
Documentation review

Service papers, original boxes, and accompanying certificates considered as part of each valuation.

What to Have Ready

  • The watch or timepiece itself
  • Original box and papers if available
  • Any service receipts or records
  • Previous valuations or purchase receipts
  • Warranty cards and accompanying literature

Having documentation to hand can support a more precise valuation, though a thorough assessment is always carried out regardless of what paperwork accompanies a piece.
